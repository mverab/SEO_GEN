<p>Merida experiences a distinct rainy season from June to October, with heavy downpours and potential flooding. These conditions can lead to water seepage, foundation issues, and damage to building materials if proper waterproofing and drainage systems are not in place.</p>

<p>Due to its coastal location, Merida is susceptible to hurricane activity during the Atlantic hurricane season.
